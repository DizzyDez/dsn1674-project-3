Our History

Established in 1960, Louis is renowned for its food and family oriented charm. Always making our pizza fresh, we are known for our yellow yolk crust and generous helping of fresh cheeses. Well known in the community, we have made special arrangements for many of our clients, even as a set for a movie. We offer you and your family delectable meals to eat at our cozy diner or to take with you. You must try our famous pizza, fresh dough daily. From our family, to yours.
